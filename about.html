About 8 Meg Stick

Originally the sole purpose of 8 Meg Stick Records was as a mechanism for releasing the output from Don Pablo, a long since abandoned writing duo, from Wells in Somerset. This was in part because the songs they created were fabulous but also 50% of that band started 8 Meg Stick Records. In tribute to the band 8 Meg Stick is a Don Pablo track and became the moniker for the label. One of these days that track will get a release but since the inception of the label more musical projects have formed and blossomed and so the label has grown.

We don’t believe in labelling or restricting what can be released. Quality is important but not the box it can be found in. This can be supported by the two releases from Pengamax. The first is a stonewall blues banger and the second release is a E.P. consisting of ambient sound sculptures evoking cinematic landscapes. There’s a lot already in the pipeline for 2021 which promises to be our busiest year yet.

Full disclosure... 50% of Don Pablo is a percentage of each act. Including 100% of PAULI. It would be great to get other artists on the label. This won’t get you rich, but we can take care of recording, mixing and mastering as needed as well as getting your tracks in to all the major digital stores.

If you would like to discuss any creative ideas that you would like to collaborate on please get in touch. We would love to hear from you.

Best wishes

Paul
